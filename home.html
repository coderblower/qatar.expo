OVERVIEW

Celebrating 50 Years of Bangladesh-Qatar Diplomatic Relations

The Bangladesh Trade-Remittance Expo and Cultural Festival-2024 marks the 50th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Bangladesh and Qatar. Held from December 15-18, 2024 in Doha, the event celebrates Bangladesh's Victory Day and Qatar's National Day. Co-hosted by the Bangladesh Embassy and Hello Superstars, UK, and managed by Ad Point, it features a Trade-Real Estate & Remittance Expo, a Grand Concert, and an Investment & Business Summit. The festival promotes bilateral trade, investment, and cultural exchange, with participation from Bangladeshi companies in garments, food, real estate, and tourism, fostering future cooperation between the two nations.
