OVERVIEW

Trade-Remittance Expo, a premier B2B trade show and networking event scheduled for December 17, 18, and 19, 2024. This expo aims to forge stronger business ties between Qatar and Bangladesh while addressing critical issues in the remittance sector, such as combating illegal remittance channels. This event is a collaborative effort between Hello Super Stars and the Bangladesh High Commission, Doha, Qatar. Our objective is to leverage this platform to promote Bangladesh’s trade potentials, exhibiting exportable products, foster investment opportunities, enhance business-to-business (B2B) networks, and support initiatives to strengthen remittance flow through legal channels. By bringing together buyers, suppliers, investors, and key market leaders from Qatar, Bangladesh, and the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries, the Bangladesh Trade-Remittance Expo will serve as a catalyst for enduring partnerships and mutual prosperity.

Trade Show

Exhibitors from Bangladesh will showcase a range of products, including handicrafts, foods, textiles, garments, electronics, agricultural products, services, IT products, financial securities, and real estate properties aiming to create opportunities for partnerships with buyers across Qatar and the Gulf region.

Networking Sessions

Dedicated B2B meetings and networking opportunities will allow attendees to connect, discuss potential partnerships, and explore new markets.

Recognition Ceremony

A special segment to honor businesses, individuals, and organizations that have contributed significantly to strengthening economic ties between Bangladesh and Qatar, with an emphasis on trade, investment, and remittances.

Why You Should Attend?

  • Promote Bangladeshi Products and Services: Showcase a diverse range of Bangladeshi products and services to potential buyers in Qatar aiming to facilitate trade partnerships and export opportunities.
  • Enhance Remittance Flows: Address the critical issues of remittance flow by promoting legal remittance channels and discouraging illegal alternatives, aiming to protect the interests of Bangladeshi expatriates and their families.
  • Foster Business-to-Business (B2B) Connections: Create an enabling platform for businesses in Bangladesh and Qatar to connect with new buyers, suppliers, and investment opportunities, facilitating sustainable trade relations.
  • Promote Investment Opportunities : Highlight business and investment opportunities in Bangladesh, encouraging Qatar-based and regional investors to explore avenues for economic growth in one of South Asia's most promising emerging markets.
  • Celebrate and Recognize Business Contributions: Celebrate the success of individuals and corporations that have made significant contributions to the economies of both Bangladesh and Qatar.
