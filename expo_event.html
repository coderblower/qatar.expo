OVERVIEW

Celebrating 50 Years of Bangladesh-Qatar Diplomatic Relations

Trade-Real Estate & Remittance Expo Trade-Real Estate & Remittance Expo is to promote Bangladesh trade and enhance the remittance flow from Qatar to Bangladesh. This program will be held on 15, 16, 17 December 2024, in this expo we will promote Bangladeshi products and services to the potential buyers not only from Qatar but also from entire gulf countries. We will strongly promote the remittance issue to stump out the touts who are encouraging Bangladeshi Expatriate to remit through illegal channel.

  • Trade-Real Estate & Remittance Expo

  • Venue: Raffels, Doha (Proposed)
  • Date: 15, 16, 17 December, 2024
  • Time: 10.00 am to 8.00 pm
