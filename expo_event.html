OVERVIEW

Trade-Remittance Expo will be a premier B2B trade show and networking event celebrating the National Day of Qatar and the Victory Day of Bangladesh. Scheduled for December 17, 18, and 19, 2024. This expo aims to forge stronger business ties between Qatar and Bangladesh while addressing critical issues in the remittance sector, such as combating illegal remittance channels. This event is a collaborative effort between Versatilo Group and the Bangladesh High Commission of Qatar. Our shared objective is to leverage this platform to promote Bangladesh’s trade potential, exhibiting exportable product, foster investment opportunities, enhance business-to-business (B2B) networks, and support initiatives to strengthen remittance flows through legal channels. By bringing together buyers, suppliers, investors, and key market leaders from Qatar, Bangladesh, and the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries, the Bangladesh Trade-Remittance Expo will serve as a catalyst for enduring partnerships and mutual prosperity

Trade Expo Activities

Exibition Stalls

  • Bangladeshi businesses, particularly SMEs, will be invited to set up booths to promote their products and services.
  • Key industries to be featured include RMG Textiles, FMCG, Banks & Insurances, Financial Institutions, Real Estates, Crafts, Agriculture, and Information Technology.
  • Special sections will be dedicated to promoting remittance services and highlighting the importance of using legal channels for money transfers.

Cultural Exchange

Celebrating Bangladesh’s rich cultural heritage, the expo will also feature cultural performances, food stalls, and traditional arts to promote soft diplomacy and cultural harmony

Trade Remittance Expo Outcome

Increased Bilateral Trade

Enhanced opportunities for Bangladeshi businesses to enter new markets in the Gulf region, boosting exports to Qatar and neighboring countries.

Stronger Remittance Flow

By discouraging illegal remittance channels and promoting secure, formal methods, the expo will increase legal remittance flow from Qatar to Bangladesh, benefiting families and the economy.

Investment in Bangladesh

The expo opens doors for investment in sectors like real estate, manufacturing, technology, and finance, promoting economic growth in Bangladesh.

Networking and Collaboration

Increased collaboration between businesses from Bangladesh and Qatar, paving the way for long-term partnerships and joint ventures.

Awareness and Education

Greater awareness among expatriates and the public about the risks of illegal remittance practices and the benefits of using regulated, legal channels.
