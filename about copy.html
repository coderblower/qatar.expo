About   EVENT

Celebrating 50 Years of Bangladesh-Qatar Diplomatic Relations

The Bangladesh Trade-Remittance Expo and Cultural Festival-2024 marks the 50th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Bangladesh and Qatar. Held from December 15-18, 2024 in Doha, the event celebrates Bangladesh's Victory Day and Qatar's National Day. Co-hosted by the Bangladesh Embassy and Hello Superstars, UK, and managed by Ad Point, it features a Trade-Real Estate & Remittance Expo, a Grand Concert, and an Investment & Business Summit. The festival promotes bilateral trade, investment, and cultural exchange, with participation from Bangladeshi companies in garments, food, real estate, and tourism, fostering future cooperation between the two nations.

EXPO

Discover a vibrant expo showcasing cutting-edge innovations, products, and services from global and regional businesses. Engage with interactive displays, experience live demonstrations, and network with exhibitors across diverse industries, all designed to create impactful connections and insights.

Cultural Show

Experience captivating performances and interactive entertainment segments designed to inspire and energize. Engage with live music, cultural shows, and immersive activities, ensuring a vibrant and memorable experience for all attendees.

BUSINESS SUMMIT

Platforms for businesses to form alliances and partnerships. This Business Summit will take place in cruise ship followed by cruise and dinner. This programme will be held on 18 December 2024.
