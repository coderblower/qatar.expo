About Us

The Omniverse Chamber of Commerce (TOCC) is the global network of chambers that connect and inspire businesses to benefit real economies and communities. TOCC works with companies to create coherent solutions across the globe. The TOCC delivers tangible results in international engagement, rules and standards, and on-the-ground solutions and implementation. Chambers of commerce are essential intermediaries between government, business, and the general public.

With a variety of programs and services, TOCC is operating in almost every country, and the TOCC represents the interests of local, national, regional, bilateral, and transnational chambers of commerce and industry worldwide. The Omniverse Chamber of Commerce (TOCC) is a global organization that supports chambers in fostering private sector development, particularly in developing countries. It connects chambers to businesses based on relevancy, recognizing the importance of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the global economy.

TOCC offers trade services, practical tools, and events to help chambers deliver the best possible service to their members.

Vision

  • To drive the future of universal business for the next century through ensuring a sustainable and dynamic environment for business communities.
  • To bring companies and chambers together through membership locally, nationally and internationally to build the community for doing business universally.
  • To strengthen capacity and networking to grow and invest in sustainable businesses and industries.

    • Mission Statement

    • To connect and inspire businesses around the world, driving tangible benefits for economies and communities through international collaboration, innovative solutions, and on-the-ground implementation.
    • To foster the growth and development of the private sector, particularly in emerging markets, while championing the importance of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the global economy.

    OVERVIEW

    Celebrating 50 Years of Bangladesh-Qatar Diplomatic Relations

    The Bangladesh Trade-Remittance Expo and Cultural Festival-2024 marks the 50th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Bangladesh and Qatar. Held from December 15-18, 2024 in Doha, the event celebrates Bangladesh's Victory Day and Qatar's National Day. Co-hosted by the Bangladesh Embassy and Hello Superstars, UK, and managed by Ad Point, it features a Trade-Real Estate & Remittance Expo, a Grand Concert, and an Investment & Business Summit. The festival promotes bilateral trade, investment, and cultural exchange, with participation from Bangladeshi companies in garments, food, real estate, and tourism, fostering future cooperation between the two nations.
