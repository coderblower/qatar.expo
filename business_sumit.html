OVERVIEW

Celebrating 50 Years of Bangladesh-Qatar Diplomatic Relations

The Bangladesh Trade-Remittance Expo and Cultural Festival-2024 marks the 50th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Bangladesh and Qatar. Held from December 17-21, 2024 in Doha, the event celebrates Bangladesh's Victory Day and Qatar's National Day. Organized By BAgnladesh Embassy, Qatar. Supported by Hello Stars , The Universe Chamber of Commerce & VERSATILO GROUP.
It features a Trade- Remittance Expo, a Grand Cultural Concert, and an Investment & Business Summit. The festival promotes bilateral trade, investment, and cultural exchange, with participation from Bangladeshi companies in garments, food, real estate, and tourism, fostering future cooperation between the two nations.

What is the Investment & Business Summit?

Join us for an inspiring event dedicated to sustainability

Experience firsthand the innovative projects of pioneering startups and connect with like-minded investors who are passionate about sustainable innovation.

The Investment & Business Summit-2024 is going to be organized under the overall theme of Investing in Sustainable Development, the Summit will encourage diasporas & global investors to promote “Made in Bangladesh” and to “Improve Trade and Investment” between Bangladesh & developed countries in the new era of global shortage of Energy, Climate Change and Raising Cost of Living. Trade and Investment stakeholders will be invited to brainstorm solution‐oriented initiatives and build global partnerships. This Investment & Business Summit will gather the business owners, leaders or experts from various industries in Bangladesh to exchange ideas to attract investors from around the world. The goal of this business summit is to share ideas, knowledge, explore new trends, and foster collaboration among participants to trigger export and import between Bangladesh and developed countries. Prominent investors from USA, UK, UAE, and other Gulf Countries are already invited to this summit. This is wonderful opportunity for the emerging startups in Bangladesh to get investment to their innovative projects.
